How to Play

Creating a Room: Click "Create Room" to generate a unique 6-character code. Share this code with friends on your local network. Once everyone joins, you can start the game.

Joining a Room: Click "Join Room" and enter the 6-character code provided by the host. You'll receive a unique Tambola ticket and wait for the host to begin the game.

Gameplay: The host picks numbers (1-90) and all players mark them on their tickets. Be the first to claim winning patterns like Early Five, Top Line, or Full House!
